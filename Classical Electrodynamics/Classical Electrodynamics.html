Classic Electromagnetism Diagram

As many Undergrad student, I strugled through Classical Eletrodynamics.

I studied a lot and I did this very nice diagram, which helps you to locate yourself inside Electromagnetism. It makes easy to see "where you are".

The diagram was based on the book Introduction to Electrodynamics by David J. Griffiths, 3rd ed. and the equation numbers follow the equation number in the book (some equations that aren't in the book have a generic number).

Note that red links show some kind of restriction. The suggested paper size for printing is A1.
